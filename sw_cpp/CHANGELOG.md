## Starwind Community Patch Project Changelog

#### Version 1.0

Replace crashy meshes identified with [Normal Maps For Everything](https://www.nexusmods.com/morrowind/mods/52567).
Also, replace the Gamorrean Axe.

# Version 1.1

1. Initial version including an omwaddon, generated by [Starwind-Builder](https://gitlab.com/modding-openmw/Starwind-Builder)
1. Fixed missing bodypart on the junkyard helm so it appears on you now
1. Lok is no longer deleted when you use the freighter buttons
1. Allow spellmaking & enchanting on loading droids and secuirty droids
1. All blaster pistols now use blaster instead of rifle rounds
1. Fix Nisav never being enabled for her quest
1. Re-enable view switching when force-exiting space

# Version 1.15

1. Reverted bad fix for shielded blaster pistols as being pistols broke their animations
1. Correct turn undead effect on lightsabers to be damage health instead
1. Allow enchanting with the fire damage effect (make your own blasters)
1. Speeder and can-cell mounts are now constant effects
1. Minor fix for Attend me compatibility that prevents pre-taris shade from following you to tatooine and duping herself
1. Re-add a missing fenelar armor piece which was deleted during development; it's now possible to complete the entire armor set!
1. Replace the rabbit mesh, correcting its size

# Version 1.2

1. Publish on S3St4sh

# Version 1.3

1. Fix meshes with non-orthogonal rotation matrices, whose collision boxes were not generated properly in openmw 0.49
1. Include replacements for Lua modules and Oxanium font

# Version 1.31

1. Include galactic basic font

# Version 1.32

1. Include fixed replacement textures for UI borders
2. Include a silent copy and Low poly copy of Rickoff's upgraded bantha models, optimized by the awesome aliens mod and silenced by me

# Version 1.4

1. Include a replacement for Meshes/Ig/SpCarRep.nif which prevents the navmeshtool from breaking by preventing generation of over 115 million unnecessary navmesh tiles
1. Replace some more meshes with collisionless ones to further optimize ingame and navmesh performance
1. Replace some meshes whose ingame collision boxes no longer worked in OpenMW 0.49
1. Begin adding replacement scripts for some of Starwind's core mechanics in Lua, covering mounts and the player ship interactions, including travel
